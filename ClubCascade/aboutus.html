About Us

Welcome to ClubCascade

ClubCascade is a dynamic digital platform built to empower student communities by making college events easier to manage, discover, and engage with. We bring students and organizers closer together by streamlining event hosting and participation.

Whether you're a student looking for events to join, or an organizer planning something extraordinary, ClubCascade is here to simplify your journey. With an intuitive interface, real-time updates, and smart integrations, our platform ensures that no event goes unnoticed.

We're proudly based at MIT ADT University, and we aim to bring innovation, creativity, and collaboration to campus life.
